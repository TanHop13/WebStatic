Our Drinks

KATAKO coffee menu is designed to provide the perfect coffee experience for every diner. From traditional coffees such as strong espresso, black coffee, to modern variations such as cappuccino, latte or macchiato, each drink is made from carefully selected, carefully roasted coffee beans. In addition, the menu also has attractive cold coffee drinks such as cold brew and iced americano, ideal for hot days. In addition to focusing on coffee, we also serve delicious teas, fresh juices and smoothies, along with pastries and snacks, creating a rich and diverse menu.
